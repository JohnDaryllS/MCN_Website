FOCCUS REVOLVING FUND FACILITY

The federation LINKS COOPERATIVES WITH EXCESS LIQUIDITY to COOPERATIVES THAT ARE IN NEED OF CREDIT TO FINANCE EXPANSION OF OPERATIONS.

The Federation offers competitive interest rates for savings deposits and loans to its members. Through prudent savings and credit management practices, the federation seeks to maximize the gains accruing to the cooperatives that participate in the revolving fund program, either as inventor or borrower.
