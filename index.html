FOCCUS stands for Finance Organization achieving Certified Union Standards. It is owned by MCN by virtue of the Deed of Transfer from WOCCU. The use of trademark is regulated by MCN. Cooperative who fail to maintain the prudential standards set by the federation are barred from using the trademark.
